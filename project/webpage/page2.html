Best Restaurants

  1. Kathmandu
    1. Fire and Ice
      • Situated on a luxury shopping street, the Fire and Ice pizzeria offers the best of traditional Italian cuisine
      • Location: 219 Sanchaya Kosh, Kathmandu, Nepal
      • Number: +97714250210
    2. Bhojan Griha
      • Built as a residence for a royal priest more than 150 years ago, the restaurant serves traditional Nepali food in charming surroundings.
      • Location: Dilli Bazaar Kathmandu, Central Development Region, 44600, Nepal
      • Number: +97714416423
  2. Pokhara
    1. Fresh Elements
      • Fresh and welcoming, the restaurant, which is located below a lovely spa called Middle Path Spa, has a wide-ranging menu with lots of international options.
      • Location: Middle Path Street, Central Lakeside
      • Number: +977 61-455744
    2. Krishna's Kitchen
      • A superb Thai restaurant with a mouthwatering menu (there's papaya salad, various curries, tom yam soup, galangal chicken soup, rice and noodle dishes, plus homemade chocolate mousse for dessert, among many other dishes).
      • Location: Khapaundi Cove, Sarangkot-2
      • Number: +977 981-4104754
