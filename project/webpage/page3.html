Travel Advice + Personal Experience

How To Prep

  • Read Articles and Blogs Online
  • Ask other for tips and their advice
  • Practice basic words so communication is easier
  • Watch Videos to see how it is there
  • Learn about the culture
  • Plan out places you want to see and go to

"You meet and see new people, and your whole outlook on life changes"

"Going to a developing country from a powerful country will really teach you a lot"

What to Have

  • Clothes for all seasons
  • Cash and coins
  • Translator - book or phone
  • Hat for the beaming sun
  • Face mask for dust
  • Contact number of someone you know or a travel agency

What I learned from Travelling

There are beautfiul places all around the world, and no matter where you have travelled, you will still be in awe of the new places you see

Meeting and talking to people who expiernce a life so different from yours is the best way to grow as an indivisual

What I gained from Travelling

More confidence but at the same more self awareness and how to be humble

Freindships, Love, and Experiences I could never gain elsewhere, Life long memeories, and most importantly, understanding myself better and becoming a better version of myself
